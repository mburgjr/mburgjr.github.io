Background

Hue.edu was created by MBC Output. MBC Output was founded by Michael Burgess in 2019 with the intention of delivering software solutions across a wide-range of applications with the goal of improving quality of life for consumers worldwide. Specifically, Hue.edu was designed to enable international access to basic math, science, and engineering subjects. The website is fully visual, containing no words outside of this paragraph and its title. It is intended to provide a language-less and intuitive educational experience for users of any background. The website's lack of words may prove difficult for some learners. However, we hope that each student takes time with each lesson (or page), do not rush yourself and seek other resources if necessary. With hope, one can obtain intuition for complicated mathematical relationships through the site's painting-like approach which can then be efficiently applied to day-to-day life.
